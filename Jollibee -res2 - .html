Jollibee

2284 Taft Ave, Malate, Manila, 1004 Metro Manila

About

Jollibee is a Filipino multinational chain of fast-food restaurants that originated in the Philippines. Some of Jollibee's most popular offerings include the Chickenjoy, which is a crispy fried chicken, and the Jolly Spaghetti, a sweet-style spaghetti dish loaded with slices of ham, ground meat, and hotdogs. The chain also serves burgers, fries, rice meals, and a range of desserts and breakfast items.
